1st place winner of the 2025 Summer Game Jam!!!

Take a nice long musical break in Tea Time. Tea Time is a randomized rhythm game with light management elements.

Rhythm games are great, but once you get used to the track, you can kinda turn your brain off and rely on muscle memory, but Tea Time introduces the random ingredients mechanic to keep you and your brain alert. Tea Time's management elements are pretty simple and light, but simple and light can be a bit much when its flying towards you at a high BPM.

this game was made for a game jam but i may keep updating it
